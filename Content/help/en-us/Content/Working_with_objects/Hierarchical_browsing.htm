Hierarchical browsing

Content Manager presents many of the lists in hierarchical format. This is due to the nature of the data with single items, groups and group members.

Opening and closing sub-levels

When you open a window containing a hierarchical list, some items may have a Plus symbol or a Minus symbol in front of them.

The plus symbol indicates there are sub-levels:

  • Click the plus symbol to open the sub-levels
  • Click the minus symbol to close the sub-levels
